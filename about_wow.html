World of Warcraft (WoW) is a mmorpg released in 2004 by Blizzard Entertainment. An action of World of Warcraft takes place within the Warcraft world of Azeroth, approximately four years after the events at the conclusion of Blizzard's Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne.
Now WoW has almost 14 years and it released 7 expansions. World of WoW is very big with great community which will help in almost all situations. Locations are very varied and beautiful.
This MMORPG offers 12 classes, 19 races and much more.
